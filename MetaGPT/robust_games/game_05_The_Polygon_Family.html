Welcome!

Regular polygons are shapes with all sides equal and all angles equal. They belong to different "families" based on the number of sides.

Triangle: 3 sides

Square: 4 sides

Pentagon: 5 sides

Hexagon: 6 sides

Heptagon: 7 sides

Octagon: 8 sides
